Programs

Building pathways through bowling

Our programs are designed to support skill development, mentorship, and access—so youth can thrive in bowling and beyond.

Youth Development

Structured coaching, practice plans, and mentorship to help bowlers grow fundamentals and confidence.

  • Weekly or monthly training sessions
  • Goal setting and progress tracking
  • Positive mentorship and leadership development

Education

Bowling education that blends technical knowledge with life skills: discipline, focus, teamwork, and resilience.

  • Fundamentals and lane play instruction
  • Sportsmanship and mental game support
  • Workshops on preparation and healthy habits

Scholarships

Financial support to help youth access camps, tournaments, training, and educational opportunities.

  • Needs-based assistance
  • Performance and commitment recognition
  • Support for travel, entry fees, and education

Community Events

Inclusive events that bring families, mentors, and local partners together to support youth development.

  • Clinics and mentorship meetups
  • Community tournaments and fundraisers
  • Equipment drives and volunteer days
