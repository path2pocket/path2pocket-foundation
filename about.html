Our story, values, and leadership

Path2Pocket Foundation exists to remove barriers and build pathways—so young bowlers can develop skills, gain mentorship, and access scholarships and equipment support.

Our Story

Bowling can be a powerful vehicle for personal growth—teaching discipline, resilience, teamwork, and focus. But too often, talented youth face barriers: limited access to coaching, the cost of equipment, and a lack of consistent mentorship.

Path2Pocket Foundation was formed to close those gaps. We partner with coaches, volunteers, families, and community supporters to provide structured development opportunities and real, measurable support.

What we believe

  • Every young person deserves a chance to grow through sport.
  • Mentorship matters as much as training.
  • Access to equipment and lane time should not depend on income.
  • Scholarships can expand what’s possible—on the lanes and in life.
